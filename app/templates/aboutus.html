About Infinitech

We’re a passionate team providing cutting-edge IT solutions, from hardware repairs to custom-built systems, software development, and network services.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize IT services, Infinitech has become one of New Zealand's leading IT solutions providers. From our early days of providing hardware repairs to now offering a full suite of IT services, we pride ourselves on delivering innovative solutions that enhance productivity and efficiency for businesses and individuals alike.

Over the years, our team has grown, and so have the challenges we tackle. Whether it's designing custom-built systems, developing tailored software solutions, or offering secure and efficient network services, we are here to solve the toughest IT problems. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide innovative, reliable, and cost-effective IT solutions that empower businesses and individuals to achieve their full potential. We aim to deliver services that are not only efficient but also sustainable, ensuring long-term success for our clients.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading provider of IT solutions in New Zealand, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We envision a future where technology empowers every business and individual to thrive in an increasingly digital world.
